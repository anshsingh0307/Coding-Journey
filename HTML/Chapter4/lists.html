Steps to bake a cake

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Grease and prepare the cake tin
  3. Mix dry ingredients
  4. Cream butter and sugar
  5. Add eggs (or substitute)
  6. Add flavoring
  7. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  8. Pour batter into cake tin
  9. Bake in the oven
  10. Check doneness with a toothpick
  11. Cool the cake
  12. Decorate if desired
